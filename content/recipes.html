Baked Potatoes

  1. If using a conventional oven, preheat to 350Â°F.
  2. Scrub one (or more) large potato(es).
  3. Use a fork to poke a fair number of holes in the potato so that it doesn't explode.
  4. Optionally coat the potato with oil and salt.
  5. Bake for about an hour or microwave until done (time depends on microwave power).
  6. Make a lengthwise incision deep into one side of the potato and squeeze so that it opens.
  7. Top with salt, pepper, sour cream, cheese, bacon, and/or any other preferred toppings.

Boiled Potatoes

  1. Scrub several small to medium potatoes.
  2. Cut into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Put the pieces into an appropriately-sized pot and and more than cover them with water.
  4. Put them on the stove and apply maximum heat.
  5. Turn down the heat as necessary to prevent boiling over.
  6. Remove from heat when the desired consistency has been acheived.
  7. Drain water from pot.

Mashed Potatoes

  1. Make boiled potatoes.
  2. Add a pat of butter and a large splash of milk.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and any other desired seasonings.
  4. Mash well.
